The Programme

Phase 1 — Foundation & Ethics M1–M5 · ~95 min

Build the shared vocabulary. Understand what AI is, what Gemini does, and how to use it safely — including the Zero Trust guardrails and the TCF prompting framework that turns AI from a guessing machine into a reliable co-pilot.

Foundation
M1 · M2 · M3 · M4 · M5 Webinar or On-site Open to All

Phase 2 — Core Tools Deep Dive M6–M9 · ~3.5 hrs

Master the four AI tools in your Vopak account: the Gemini side panel in Gmail & Docs, the Web App for heavy analysis, Gemini on Mobile for field staff, and NotebookLM as your team's grounded knowledge base.

Core Tools
M6 · M7 · M8 · M9 Half-day or Full-day Requires Phase 1

Phase 3 — Automate & Build M10–M11 · ~4 hrs

Go from user to builder. Create custom Gems — pre-loaded AI colleagues with a fixed role, rules, and knowledge base. Then automate personal workflows with Workspace Studio and bridge team workflows with Gemini-written Apps Script.
