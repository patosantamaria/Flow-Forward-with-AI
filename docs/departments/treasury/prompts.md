## ⚡ Starter Prompts (60-second wins — try these first!)

> These require zero setup. Just open Gemini and type. Perfect for your first day.

1. **"What are the key risks in a rising interest rate environment for a company with significant floating-rate debt?"** — Instant macro briefing.
2. **"Summarize this bank covenant clause and flag any restrictions on new borrowing."** — Cut legal-financial complexity in under a minute.
3. **"Draft a short internal update on current FX exposure for our EUR/USD positions."** — Structured treasury communication, fast.

## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Cash Flow Forecast Reconciliation (Sheets)

Open Google Sheets with CFF submissions from multiple Business Units.

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Treasury Analyst. Compare these 12-month CFF submissions from two Business Units. Identify: (1) naming convention inconsistencies, (2) line items where variance exceeds 5%, (3) formatting errors that would break the consolidation template. Present as a reconciliation table: Line Item | BU1 Value | BU2 Value | Variance % | Issue."

### Exercise 2: Liquidity Position Summary (Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Senior Vopak Treasury Manager. Draft a weekly liquidity position update for the CFO. Include: available credit facilities and utilisation, cash on hand by region, projected net cash movement over the next 30 days, and any covenant headroom concerns. Tone: concise, factual, executive-ready."

### Exercise 3: Inter-Company Loan Memo (Gmail/Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Treasury Analyst. Draft an inter-company loan memo between two Vopak entities. Include: loan amount, currency, interest rate (EURIBOR + 150bps), repayment schedule, and governing law. Flag any sections that require Legal review before execution."

## Gemini Web App — Advanced Analysis

### Exercise 4: Bank Facility Comparison

Upload two term sheets from different banks.

> **Prompt:** "Compare these two bank facility term sheets. Create a side-by-side table showing: facility amount, pricing (margin + fees), covenant package, availability period, and key conditions precedent. Highlight the top 3 differences that would influence our bank selection decision."

### Exercise 5: FX Hedging Strategy Brief

> **Prompt:** "Research current EUR/USD hedging instruments available to corporate treasurers. Summarise: forward contracts, options, and cross-currency swaps. For each: explain the mechanism, cost structure, and when Vopak would typically use it. Present as a 1-page treasury briefing."
