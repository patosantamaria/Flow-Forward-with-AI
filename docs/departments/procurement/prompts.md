## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Vendor TCO Comparison — Ariba Evaluation (Sheets)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Senior Vopak Sourcing Buyer. Evaluate these three vendor quotes for a Vapor Treatment Unit using the Ariba E-Sourcing 'apples to apples' methodology. Separate the analysis into: (1) Technical Scorecard (compliance certifications, material specifications, safety ratings — weighted 40%), (2) Commercial Scorecard (unit price, lead time, warranty terms, after-sales support — weighted 60%). Calculate Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) including freight, installation, and 5-year maintenance estimates. Present as a comparison table with a final recommendation keyed to our SEM (Service Engagement Manual) framework."

### Exercise 2: Terms Sheet Generation from Email Thread (Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Senior Vopak Procurement Manager. I will paste a complete email negotiation thread with a Tier 1 strategic vendor. Extract: (1) the final agreed positions on each commercial point, (2) specific deviations from Vopak's General Terms & Conditions (GTCs) regarding liability exposure, payment terms, and warranty periods, (3) leverage points we still hold, (4) outstanding open items. Format as a Terms Sheet with columns: Clause | Vopak GTC Standard | Vendor Position | Agreed Position | Risk Level. Flag any deviations that require Legal review."

### Exercise 3: Supplier Performance Communication (Gmail)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Procurement Manager. Draft an email to a supplier whose performance has slipped below our KPI targets: their registration lead time is averaging 8 workdays (target: <5) and their first-time PO invoice match rate has dropped to 45% (target: 70%, minimum: 50%). Structure: (1) cite specific KPIs with data, (2) reference the SEM framework, (3) request a corrective action plan within 10 business days, (4) note that continued non-compliance may trigger a vendor performance review. Tone: professional, factual, collaborative but firm."
