# Procurement — "The Supply Chain Sprint" (Phase 3 Capstone)


---

## The Scenario

The ApexChem HMD project requires emergency procurement: PTFE gaskets (6-8 week lead time), a VTU unit (EUR 450K), and trace heating upgrades (EUR 180K). The Project Phoenix budget is locked, but **Maintenance** just discovered urgent material replacements needed. Your job: find the best vendors, validate compliance, and accelerate the procurement timeline.

---

## Your Mission (Search → Apply → Refine)

### 1. Search (15 min)

> **Prompt:** "Research suppliers for industrial PTFE gaskets rated for HMD chemical exposure. Identify 3 potential suppliers based on: product compatibility, lead time, pricing, and quality certifications (ISO/EN standards). Present as a supplier shortlist."

### 2. Apply (30 min)

> **Prompt:** "Draft a Request for Quotation for the following items: (1) PTFE gaskets, HMD-compatible, Qty: 48 units, (2) Vapor Treatment Unit, rated for HMD vapor pressure, Qty: 1. Include: technical specifications, delivery requirement (6 weeks max), evaluation criteria (50% technical fit, 30% price, 20% lead time), and compliance requirements."

### 3. Refine (20 min)

> **Prompt:** "Create a procurement compliance checklist ensuring this RFQ meets Vopak's procurement policy for CAPEX exceeding EUR 500K. Include: required approvals, vendor qualification steps, and documentation requirements."

---

## Cross-Department Check (at 60 min)

> "Get the final specifications from **Maintenance** — they've identified the exact gasket types needed. Also confirm with **Finance** that the CAPEX approval path is clear."

---

## Expected Deliverable

1. Supplier shortlist with comparison
2. Request for Quotation document
3. Procurement compliance checklist
