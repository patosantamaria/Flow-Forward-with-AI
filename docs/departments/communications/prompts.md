## ⚡ Starter Prompts (60-second wins — try these first!)

## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Flow 28 Internal Announcement (Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Internal Communications Manager. Draft an intranet announcement for the upcoming quarterly Town Hall. Theme: 'Flow 28 — From 9 Incidents to Zero.' Cover: (1) the strategic targets (Safety: zero incidents by 2028, NPS: from 60 to 70, Free Cash Flow: €250M), (2) how the 'Flow Forward with AI' initiative directly supports these goals — give one concrete example per target, (3) frame around the three cultural pillars: Trust (trusting enterprise data security), Collaboration (sharing Gems across departments), and Courage — 'Lef' (experimenting with new prompting techniques). Tone: inspiring, authentic, direct. Keep under 300 words."

### Exercise 2: MOVES Program Change Communication (Gmail)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Change Communications Lead supporting the MOVES program rollout. Draft an email to all terminal staff preparing them for the transition from legacy PEPI to the new MyService/Oracle Cloud Services (OCS) suite. Structure: (1) What is changing and when, (2) Why it matters (One Vopak Experience standardization), (3) What employees need to do (complete e-learning modules via MLO by date X), (4) What support is available (AI assistants in NotebookLM loaded with the new SOPs — 'just ask how to log a pipeline transfer in the new system'). Tone: empathetic, practical, change-positive. Include a Q&A section with 3 anticipated questions."

### Exercise 3: Crisis Holding Statement (Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Corporate Communications Manager. Draft a press holding statement in response to a media inquiry about a fuel oil spill at a Netherlands terminal (approximately 2,500 kg, classified as TIER 2 LOPC — Loss of Primary Containment). The statement must: (1) acknowledge the incident factually, (2) confirm no injuries and no ongoing environmental risk, (3) reference the immediate containment actions taken, (4) cite Vopak's track record and the Flow 28 zero-incident commitment, (5) state that a formal root cause analysis under the Vopak Way standards is underway. Tone: transparent, responsible, measured. Keep under 150 words."
