# Communications — "The Narrative Builder" (Phase 3 Capstone)

> **Track E: ENABLE** | 75-minute capstone challenge

---

## The Scenario

The CEO is about to announce the ApexChem partnership and the company's broader New Energy strategy. Communications must develop a **3-month transition narrative** that positions this as a strategic milestone — not just a new contract. The Works Council and employees need to feel included, not blindsided.

---

## Your Mission (Search → Apply → Refine)

### 1. Search (15 min)

> **Prompt:** "Research how leading industrial companies have communicated major strategic pivots to their workforce. Identify: (1) best practices for change communication, (2) common mistakes that trigger employee resistance, (3) successful narrative frameworks used in energy transition announcements."

### 2. Apply (30 min)

> **Prompt:** "Draft a 3-month internal communications plan for the ApexChem announcement and New Energy strategy. Structure: Month 1 (CEO Launch + Town Hall), Month 2 (Department Deep Dives + FAQ Distribution), Month 3 (Progress Updates + Employee Stories). For each month: key message, channel (email/intranet/town hall), audience, owner."

> **Prompt:** "Write the CEO's 2-minute launch script for the town hall. Key themes: Vopak's heritage in energy storage, the natural evolution toward New Energies, how ApexChem opens the door to LOHC and Green Ammonia, and what it means for employees. Tone: visionary, inclusive, authentic."

### 3. Refine (20 min)

Polish both deliverables.

---

## Cross-Department Check (at 60 min)

> "Get the reskilling timeline from **HR** — your communications plan must align with their workforce development milestones."

---

## Expected Deliverable

1. 3-month internal communications plan
2. CEO town hall launch script
