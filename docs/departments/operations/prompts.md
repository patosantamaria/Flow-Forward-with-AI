## ⚡ Starter Prompts (60-second wins — try these first!)

> These require zero setup. Just open Gemini and type. Perfect for your first day.

1. **"Summarize this shift log in 3 bullets."** — Paste any shift handover notes. Done in 10 seconds.
2. **"Draft a short email to the team about today's weather advisory and its impact on operations."** — Get a professional email in one prompt.
3. **"What are the top 5 safety checks I should do before a truck loading operation?"** — Instant checklist from general knowledge.

## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Shift Handover Summary (Docs)

Open Google Docs. Paste raw shorthand shift notes (or use sample notes provided).

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Operations Superintendent. Structure these raw shift notes into a formal Daily Shift Handover Report. Sections: (1) Safety Incidents & Observations, (2) Equipment Status — especially any defeated safety-critical equipment, (3) Active Permits to Work (PtW) with status, (4) Ongoing Jetty Activities, (5) Pending Maintenance Tasks. Flag any items that require immediate attention from the incoming shift."

### Exercise 2: PEPI Throughput Analysis (Sheets)

Open Google Sheets with a sample throughput dataset.

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Terminal Operations Analyst. Analyze this PEPI throughput data and identify: (1) any movements where pre- and post-ATG (Automatic Tank Gauging) measurements show variance above tolerance, (2) missing tolerance codes that need manual entry, (3) concurrent movements that may cause scheduling conflicts. Present as a flagged items table with columns: Movement ID | Product | Variance | Status | Action Needed."

### Exercise 3: Operational Communication (Gmail)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Shift Supervisor at a Vopak terminal. Draft an email to the Terminal Manager reporting that the ISGOTT compliance checklist for Berth 3 has been completed, but two items need follow-up: the fire-fighting foam system pressure is below minimum, and the manifold alignment requires re-verification before the next vessel arrives. Tone: factual, urgent but measured, with clear next steps."

## Gemini Web App — Advanced Analysis

### Exercise 4: Throughput Analysis

Upload weekly throughput data.

> **Prompt:** "Analyze this weekly throughput data for the past 3 months. Identify trends in: tank utilization rates, average turnaround time, and peak demand periods. What operational improvements would increase throughput by 10% without additional infrastructure? Present as a recommendation memo."

## NotebookLM — Document Synthesis

### Exercise 5: Procedure Comparison

Upload the current SOP and a proposed revised SOP.

> **Prompt:** "Compare these two versions of the operating procedure. List every change made, categorize each as: Safety Enhancement, Efficiency Improvement, or Regulatory Compliance. Flag any changes that could increase operational risk."
