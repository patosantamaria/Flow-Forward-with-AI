# Operations — "Terminal Optimizer" (Phase 3 Capstone)


---

## The Scenario

Your terminal is preparing to receive ApexChem's **HMD product** for the first time. The Operations team must ensure the terminal is ready — but the existing procedures, scheduling, and safety protocols were designed for conventional products, not HMD.

The Terminal Manager needs a comprehensive operational readiness assessment **before the first truck arrives**.

---

## Your Mission (Search → Apply → Refine)

### 1. Search (15 min)

Locate and synthesize relevant safety data, handling procedures, and scheduling constraints for HMD.

> **Prompt:** "Based on the HMD Safety Data Sheet, identify all operational requirements that differ from our standard product handling: storage temperature, PPE requirements, loading/unloading procedures, emergency response, and incompatible materials. Present as a gap analysis against our current generic procedures."

### 2. Apply (30 min)

Create an operational readiness checklist and a revised truck scheduling plan.

> **Prompt:** "Based on the gap analysis, create: (1) An operational readiness checklist for HMD reception (infrastructure, PPE, procedures, personnel training), (2) A revised truck loading schedule that accounts for the additional safety steps required for HMD. Format the checklist as: Item | Current Status | Required Status | Action Needed | Owner."

### 3. Refine (20 min)

Draft a 2-minute toolbox talk script for supervisors to brief terminal operators.

> **Prompt:** "Write a 2-minute toolbox talk script that a shift supervisor would read to terminal operators before the first HMD truck arrives. Cover: what HMD is, why it's different from current products, specific PPE required, key safety procedures, and emergency contacts. Language: simple, direct, no jargon."

### 4. Optional Stretch Goal

Transform the toolbox script into a video training presentation.
