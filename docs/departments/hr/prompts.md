## ⚡ Starter Prompts (60-second wins — try these first!)

> These require zero setup. Just open Gemini and type. Perfect for your first day.

1. **"Draft a welcoming onboarding email for a new team member joining next Monday."** — Professional, warm, done instantly.
2. **"Summarize this HR policy document in 5 key points an employee should know."** — Paste any policy, get the essentials.
3. **"List 5 good interview questions for a project manager role."** — Instant preparation for hiring.

## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Onboarding Journey Design (Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak HR Business Partner. Draft a 30-day onboarding checklist for a new Terminal Operations Engineer joining the AME region. Include: Week 1 (IT setup via CloudM, SHEQ induction, IS-Safe tablet training), Week 2 (system access — MyService, PEPI, Maximo), Week 3 (shadowing shifts with a buddy), Week 4 (first independent shift). For each item, specify: Owner (HR/IT/Operations), Prerequisites, and Completion Criteria. Align with the 'Flow Forward' culture."

### Exercise 2: Learning Path with AI Milestones (Sheets)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak People & Culture Specialist. Design a 6-month learning path for a new engineer. Structure as a weekly plan with columns: Week | Topic | Format (e-learning/workshop/mentoring) | Resource | Milestone. Include AI literacy milestones: Month 2 — complete Phase 1 'Foundation & Ethics' training, Month 4 — build first personal Gem, Month 6 — present a 'Time Saved' ROI to their manager."

### Exercise 3: Employee Communication (Gmail)

> **Prompt:** "Act as the HR Director. Draft an internal announcement email about the launch of the AI Champions Network. Explain: what it is (peer-to-peer AI mentoring across departments), who can join (anyone who has completed Phase 2 training), the benefits (recognition as AI Pioneer, priority access to advanced tools, direct input to the CoE), and how to nominate yourself. Tone: exciting but professional. Keep under 200 words."
