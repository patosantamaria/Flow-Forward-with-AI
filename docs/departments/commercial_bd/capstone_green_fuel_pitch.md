## The Scenario

A major global shipping line — **OceanGreen Maritime** — has sent a surprise enquiry to Vopak Singapore. They need **50,000 cbm of storage** for a new **Green Fuel blend** (Green Methanol) starting Q3. They are also talking to your **neighbours on Jurong Island**.

The enquiry looks attractive — but buried in the 20-page requirements document are clauses that could cost Vopak millions if you don't catch them.

**Both teams receive the same enquiry. Both build a pitch. Only one wins "Deal of the Day."**

## The Hidden Traps (Facilitator Only — DO NOT reveal!)

Embedded in the OceanGreen enquiry:

1. **"Most Favoured Customer" clause** — OceanGreen demands that any price reduction offered to another customer must be extended to them. This would undermine Vopak's entire pricing strategy.
2. **Unlimited liability for product contamination** — The standard Vopak position limits liability to the value of product in the tank. OceanGreen's clause shifts ALL contamination liability to Vopak with no cap.
3. **Exclusive berthing priority** — OceanGreen wants guaranteed 4-hour berthing windows, blocking other customers during peak periods. This would reduce Vopak's overall throughput.

> Teams that find all 3 traps AND propose counter-language earn maximum points.

## The Mission — Both Teams (Parallel)

### Task 1: Market Intelligence & Feasibility ⚡ REQUIRED

**Step 1 — NotebookLM (10 min):**
Upload all Data Room documents into a NotebookLM notebook.

> **Prompt:** "Cross-reference the OceanGreen enquiry against our terminal capabilities. Can we accommodate 50,000 cbm of Green Methanol? What infrastructure modifications would be required? Cite specific documents."

> **Bonus:** "Generate an Audio Overview of the market intelligence documents — listen while you plan your approach."

**Step 2 — Deep Research (10 min):**
Use Gemini Deep Research to investigate the Green Fuel market:

> **Prompt:** "Research Green Methanol storage requirements for maritime bunkering in Southeast Asia. Focus on: (1) material compatibility with existing carbon steel tanks — does it require nitrogen blanketing or specialised seals? (2) Singapore MPA regulations for Green Bunkering (latest circulars), (3) competitor activity in Jurong Island for Green Fuel storage. Cite specific data points."

> **Deliverable 1 — Technical Feasibility Note (REQUIRED):** A 1-page summary of Green Methanol storage requirements, infrastructure gaps, and regulatory requirements.

### Task 2: Trap Detection & Competitive Positioning ⚡ REQUIRED

**Step 3 — Contract Analysis (10 min):**

> **Prompt:** "Compare this customer enquiry against Vopak's standard Terms & Conditions. Identify every clause that deviates from standard. For each deviation: describe the risk to Vopak, estimate the potential financial impact, and propose counter-language. Classify each as: Acceptable / Negotiate / Reject."

> **Deliverable 2 — Risk & Opportunity Matrix (REQUIRED):** Table showing each trap found, risk assessment, and proposed counter-position.

**Step 4 — Competitive Positioning (5 min):**

> **Prompt:** "Based on the competitive landscape brief, list 5 strategic advantages of Vopak Singapore (Banyan & Sebarok terminals) over competitors on Jurong Island for a client focused on the Green Energy transition. Emphasise: safety record (Fundamentals on Safety), jetty efficiency, sustainability credentials, and proximity to major shipping routes."

> **Deliverable 3 — The Vopak Difference (REQUIRED):** 3 "Winning Points" that differentiate Vopak — must include at least ONE point that comes from the team's own Vopak knowledge, not just AI output. This is the "human edge."

### Task 3: The Executive Pitch ⚡ REQUIRED

**Step 5 — Drafting (15 min):**

> **Deliverable 4 — Pitch Letter (REQUIRED):** "Draft a 3-paragraph executive response to OceanGreen Maritime's VP of Procurement. Paragraph 1: Acknowledge their enquiry and express strategic interest. Paragraph 2: Our value proposition — safety, location, Green Fuel readiness, and sustainability. Paragraph 3: Proposed next steps (site visit, technical review, commercial negotiation). Tone: confident, strategic, partnership-oriented — not desperate."

> **Deliverable 5 — Counter-Proposal Deck (REQUIRED):** Use the Vopak Slide Designer to create a 5-7 slide pitch deck:
>
> - Slide 1: Title — "Vopak Singapore: Your Green Fuel Partner"
> - Slide 2: Understanding Your Needs (show you've read the enquiry deeply)
> - Slide 3: The Vopak Difference (3 winning points)
> - Slide 4: Technical Readiness — Green Methanol storage capabilities
> - Slide 5: Commercial Terms (with counter-positions on traps highlighted)
> - Slide 6: Sustainability Credentials & Green Bunkering Roadmap
> - Slide 7: Next Steps & Decision Ask

### Task 4: Video Pitch 📋 OPTIONAL BONUS

> **Deliverable 6 — 2-Minute Video Pitch (OPTIONAL):** Use Google Vids to turn the slide deck into a narrated executive video. Imagine this is sent to OceanGreen's CEO who couldn't make the meeting.

### Task 5: Pushback Email 📋 OPTIONAL

> **Deliverable 7 — Strategic Pushback Email (OPTIONAL):** "Draft a senior-level email to OceanGreen addressing the 3 problematic clauses you found. Tone: collaborative but firm. Show that you want the deal, but on terms that work for both sides."

## 🏅 Scoring Rubric

| Criteria                  | Points    | What Judges Look For                                                                                                                                                          |
| :------------------------ | :-------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Trap Detection**        | /20       | Found all 3 hidden traps? Proposed counter-language?                                                                                                                          |

| **AI Tool Breadth**       | /15       | Used NotebookLM + Deep Research + Gemini + Slides? Source citations present?                                                                                                  |
