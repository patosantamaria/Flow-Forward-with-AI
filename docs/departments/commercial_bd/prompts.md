## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Deal Strategize — RFP Analysis (Docs)

Open Google Docs. Paste a sample customer RFP or deal summary.

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Senior Vopak Commercial Manager executing the Lead to Contract (L2C) Blueprint. Analyze this RFP and produce a Deal Strategize output: (1) Customer Buying Criteria (extracted directly from their language), (2) VAD Pricing Analysis — calculate Aspirational, Realistic, and Walk-away price tiers using the Value-Alternatives-Differentiated model, (3) Potential Operational Bottlenecks — flag any service requirements that must be validated by the Operations department before promising, (4) Antitrust Compliance Check — highlight any price-related language that must be reviewed against the 2025 Pricing Policy. Format as a structured Deal Strategy brief."

### Exercise 2: Commercial Master Plan (Sheets)

Open Google Sheets with sample terminal capacity and PMC data.

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Business Development Manager. Draft a Commercial Master Plan (CMP) summary for this terminal. Analyze: (1) current Product Market Combinations (PMCs) and their utilization rates, (2) expiring agreements within 12 months (flag using Terminal 360 logic), (3) capacity available for new customer acquisition, (4) strategic fit with New Energies growth targets (hydrogen, ammonia, SAF). Present as a terminal opportunity matrix with columns: PMC | Current Revenue | Utilization % | Expiry Date | Growth Potential | Strategic Fit Score."

### Exercise 3: Customer Communication — SLE-Aligned (Gmail)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Commercial Director. Draft a follow-up email to a customer after the initial L2C 'meeting report' stage. Reference: the specific service capabilities discussed, the Service Level Expectations (SLEs) we can commit to without operational validation, and a proposed next step (site visit + Operations feasibility review). Frame sustainability surcharges as an investment in green infrastructure. Ensure no language implies pricing commitments that might create antitrust exposure. Tone: partnership-focused, strategic, under 200 words."

> **Prompt:** "Cross-reference these two documents. Find every clause in the RFP that contradicts or exceeds the limits in our Pricing Matrix. Present conflicts in a table: RFP Clause | Our Limit | Risk Level (High/Medium/Low) | Recommended Response."

Upload Vopak's Standard Terms & Conditions and a customer's proposed contract terms.

> **Prompt:** "Compare these two documents. What are the top 5 areas where the customer's terms deviate from our standard? For each deviation, cite the exact clause numbers from both documents and rate the commercial risk."
