# Finance — "The Investment Radar" (Phase 3 Capstone)

> **Track E: ENABLE** | 75-minute capstone challenge

---

## The Scenario

The CFO needs two concrete investment proposals for the AME region by end of day. The ApexChem HMD deal requires CAPEX for infrastructure upgrades (VTU, trace heating, gasket replacements), but the board wants to see this in the context of a broader regional growth strategy.

Meanwhile, **Projects & Engineering** (Track B) just discovered the budget needs to be revised upward.

---

## Your Mission (Search → Apply → Refine)

### 1. Search (15 min)

> **Prompt:** "Research the chemical storage market in Southeast Asia and the Middle East. Identify: (1) products with highest growth demand (focus on new energies: LOHC, Green Ammonia), (2) regulatory trends affecting storage requirements, (3) competitor investment activity in the region. Cite specific data points."

### 2. Apply (30 min)

> **Prompt:** "Draft a CAPEX investment proposal for the ApexChem HMD infrastructure. Include: (1) Executive Summary, (2) Business Justification (strategic value of the New Energy pipeline), (3) Cost Breakdown (VTU: EUR 450K, trace heating upgrade: EUR 180K, gasket replacement: EUR 75K), (4) ROI projection over 5 years, (5) Risk factors, (6) Recommendation. Format for board presentation."

### 3. Refine (20 min)

> **Prompt:** "Create a financial summary table comparing the 'invest now' scenario vs. 'delay to Q4' scenario. Include: total cost, revenue impact, opportunity cost of delay, and risk of losing ApexChem to competitors."

---

## Cross-Department Check (at 60 min)
