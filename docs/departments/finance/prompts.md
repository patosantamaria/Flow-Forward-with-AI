## ⚡ Starter Prompts (60-second wins — try these first!)

> These require zero setup. Just open Gemini and type. Perfect for your first day.

1. **"Summarize this email thread — what's the budget ask and who needs to approve it?"** — Cut through long financial discussions instantly.
2. **"Draft a short reply declining a travel expense that exceeds the policy limit."** — Professional, polite, done in 10 seconds.
3. **"What are the key components of a variance analysis report?"** — Get a template outline from general best practice.

## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Month-End CFF Reconciliation (Sheets)

Open Google Sheets with sample Cash Flow Forecast data from two BUs.

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Treasury Analyst. Compare these two 12-month CFF submissions from different Business Units. Identify: (1) naming convention inconsistencies between the files, (2) line items where variance exceeds 5%, (3) any formatting errors that would break the consolidation template. Present as a reconciliation report with columns: Line Item | BU1 Value | BU2 Value | Variance | Issue Type."

### Exercise 2: Accrual Tracking & PO Compliance (Sheets)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Financial Controller. Analyze this PO log and flag: (1) all Purchase Orders exceeding €500 that lack a matching invoice (these require transitory bookings), (2) POs open for more than 60 days, (3) POs where the accrual amount differs from the invoiced amount by more than 10%. Format as a compliance table with recommended actions."

### Exercise 3: Financial Covenant Summary (Gmail)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Senior Vopak Treasury Analyst. Draft a brief email to the CFO summarizing the current status of our financial covenants. Our leverage ratio is currently at 2.7x against a target range of 2.5x to 3.0x. Note that the recent CAPEX acceleration for New Energies projects may push us toward the upper limit. Include: current position, headroom, key risks, and recommended guardrails. Tone: analytical, concise, executive-ready."
