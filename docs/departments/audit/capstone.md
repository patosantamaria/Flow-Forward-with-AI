# Audit — "The Compliance Watchdog" (Phase 3 Capstone)

> **Track E: ENABLE** | 75-minute capstone challenge

---

## The Scenario

The ApexChem HMD deal involves significant CAPEX (VTU: EUR 450K, trace heating: EUR 180K, gaskets: EUR 75K). Internal Audit must verify that procurement follows Vopak's CAPEX policy and that environmental permits are in order for the new product.

---

## Your Mission (Search → Apply → Refine)

### 1. Search (15 min)

> **Prompt:** "Based on Vopak's CAPEX approval policy, determine: (1) which approval level is required for a total CAPEX of EUR 705K, (2) what documentation must be submitted, (3) the typical approval timeline, (4) any special considerations for environmental or safety-related investments."

### 2. Apply (30 min)

> **Prompt:** "Draft an audit readiness checklist for the ApexChem CAPEX submission. Cover: financial documentation, vendor selection compliance, environmental permit status, Works Council consultation requirements, and board presentation quality standards. Format as: Item | Required Document | Status | Owner | Deadline."

### 3. Refine (20 min)

> **Prompt:** "Write a formal audit opinion memo recommending conditional approval of the CAPEX. Conditions: (1) environmental permit confirmation, (2) second vendor quote for VTU, (3) Works Council notification. Tone: impartial, thorough."

---

## Cross-Department Check (at 60 min)

> "Get the updated CAPEX figures from **Finance** — Projects & Engineering just revised the budget upward."

---

## Expected Deliverable

1. Audit readiness checklist
2. Conditional approval recommendation memo
