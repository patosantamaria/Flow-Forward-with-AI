# Customer Service — "The Client Shield" (Phase 3 Capstone)

> **Track A: GROW (variant)** | 75-minute capstone challenge

---

## The Scenario

**ApexChem Global** is about to become Vopak's newest HMD storage client. Your Customer Service team needs to prepare the onboarding experience — but ApexChem has a reputation for being demanding. They've already sent a list of 15 operational questions about scheduling, safety protocols, and invoicing that need answers **before the contract is signed**.

Meanwhile, your existing customers at the terminal are concerned about how the new HMD product will affect their operations.

---

## Your Mission (Search → Apply → Refine)

### 1. Search (15 min)

Use Gemini and NotebookLM to find answers to ApexChem's 15 operational questions.

> **Prompt:** "Based on these operational documents, answer the following customer questions about HMD storage: [paste questions]. For each answer, cite the specific document and section. Flag any questions that cannot be answered from the available documents."

### 2. Apply (30 min)

Create two deliverables:

- A comprehensive FAQ response document for ApexChem
- A proactive communication to existing customers about the new product

> **Prompt:** "Draft a professional customer communication explaining that a new chemical product (HMD) will be stored at the terminal. Address potential concerns about safety, scheduling impact, and environmental compliance. Tone: reassuring, transparent, factual."

### 3. Refine (20 min)

Polish both documents and prepare your pitch.

### 4. Optional Stretch Goal

Create a customer onboarding checklist template that can be reused for future new clients.

---
