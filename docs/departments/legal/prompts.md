## ⚡ Starter Prompts (60-second wins — try these first!)

> These require zero setup. Just open Gemini and type. Perfect for your first day.

1. **"Summarise this contract clause in plain English — what obligation does it create and for which party?"** — Clear legal translation in seconds.
2. **"What are the typical indemnification provisions in a terminal services agreement?"** — Instant legal framing before a negotiation.
3. **"Draft a short NDA reminder email to a counterparty who hasn't returned the signed document yet."** — Professional, firm, done.

## Gemini in Workspace — Guided Practice

### Exercise 1: Contract Clause Comparison (Docs)

Open two versions of a contract or standard Vopak template.

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Senior Vopak Legal Counsel specialising in commercial contracts. Compare these two versions of the indemnification clause. Identify: (1) material differences in liability scope, (2) any gap in mutual indemnification, (3) clauses that exceed Vopak's standard risk appetite. Present as a redline summary: Original | Proposed | Risk Level | Recommendation."

### Exercise 2: Regulatory Research Brief (Gemini Web App)

> **Prompt:** "Research the current regulatory requirements for terminal operations in [jurisdiction]. Summarise: (1) key environmental permits required, (2) data residency and privacy obligations for operational data, (3) any recent regulatory changes in the past 12 months that affect storage terminals. Present as a structured regulatory brief with source citations."

### Exercise 3: Contract Review Checklist (Docs)

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak Legal Analyst. Review this service agreement against our standard Contract Review Checklist. Flag: (1) missing dispute resolution clause, (2) any limitation of liability cap below our standard EUR 5M threshold, (3) absence of Vopak's standard Fundamental Rules compliance clause, (4) governing law deviating from Netherlands. Output: Issue | Clause Reference | Risk Level | Recommended Amendment."

## Gemini Web App — Advanced Analysis

### Exercise 4: Cross-Jurisdictional Compliance Check

Upload two contracts from different jurisdictions.

> **Prompt:** "Compare the governing law and dispute resolution provisions in these two terminal operating agreements — one for Singapore, one for the Netherlands. Identify: (1) key differences in legal framework, (2) enforceability risks if disputes arise, (3) any clauses that may be unenforceable under local law. Recommend a harmonised approach for our standard contract template."

### Exercise 5: GDPR / Data Privacy Impact

> **Prompt:** "Review this data processing agreement from a vendor. Assess: (1) whether the DPA meets GDPR Article 28 requirements for a data processor, (2) sub-processor provisions and notice requirements, (3) data breach notification timelines and their compliance with our 72-hour obligation. Flag any gaps and suggest standard remediation language."

## NotebookLM — Document Synthesis

### Exercise 6: Contract Portfolio Q&A

Upload 3-5 existing terminal operating agreements.

> **Prompt:** "Across these terminal operating agreements, what are the most common force majeure triggers? Are there any inconsistencies in how the force majeure clause is defined between agreements? Summarise in a table: Agreement | Force Majeure Triggers | Notice Period | Termination Right."
