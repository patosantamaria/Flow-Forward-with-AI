# Capstone Facilitator Guide

> **Core Philosophy:** The capstone is a high-pressure, realistic simulation. Your job is to keep the energy high and guide them when stuck.

## Timing Guide (Full Challenge: ~80 min)

1. **Introduction & Setup (5 min):** Explain the scenario, assign groups.
2. **Search Phase (15 min):** Upload Data Room files to NotebookLM, run Deep Research.
3. **Apply Phase (30 min):** The core analysis in Gemini Web App. _Inject the Simulated Update here._
4. **Refine Phase (15 min):** Finalize the analysis and pivot based on the update.
5. **Create Phase (15 min):** Use Slide Deck Creator Gem and (optional) Google Vids.

> ⚡ **Quick Challenge Mode (~30 min):** Skip the deep research. Focus purely on the Gemini Web App analysis and the 3-slide summary.

## The Mid-Challenge Simulation

At the 45-minute mark (or 15-minute mark for Quick Challenge), tell participants to ask their Capstone Facilitator Gem: _"Give me my simulated cross-department update."_ This will throw a wrench in their plan and force them to adapt quickly.

## The Debrief (10 min)

- Have 2-3 teams present their 2-minute pitches.
- Ask: "What surprised you most about using NotebookLM for this?"
- Remind them to save all their outputs to the Flow Forward Shared Drive.
