# Phase 1 Check-In Survey: How Is It Going?

> **When to deploy:** 1 week after Phase 1 (before Phase 2 on-site begins)
> **Format:** Google Forms (short link sent via email and Google Chat)
> **Purpose:** Bridge the gap between the baseline survey and post-training feedback. Identify who is experimenting, who has dropped off, and what to reinforce in Phase 2.
> **Time to complete:** ~3 minutes

---

## Questions

**Email** _(required, auto-collected)_

**Which department are you in?** _(dropdown)_

---

**Since Phase 1, have you tried using Gemini at work?**

- Yes — I use it daily now
- Yes — I tried it once or twice
- Not yet — but I plan to
- No — I haven't had time
- No — I ran into a problem

**If you tried it: what did you use it for?** _(short answer, optional)_

> _Example: "Summarizing a long email thread before a meeting."_

**If you haven't tried it yet: what's the main reason?** _(single select, optional)_

- I forgot / got busy
- I wasn't sure what to ask
- I tried and it didn't give helpful results
- I'm not sure what's allowed
- I don't have access to the tools

---

**How confident do you feel using the TCF (Persona, Task, Context, Format) prompting method?** _(1 = Not at all confident → 5 = Very confident)_

**Is there anything from Phase 1 you'd like us to revisit or clarify during Phase 2?** _(short answer)_
