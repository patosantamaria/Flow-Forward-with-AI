# Facilitator Guide: Running Department-Specific Capstones

> **Important Setup Note:** Capstones are designed to be run **per department**, not with mixed groups.

## The "Simulated Cross-Department Update"

Because participants are only working with their own department, they cannot physically check with other departments (e.g., Commercial checking with Engineering or Operations with SHEQ) as they would in real life.

Instead, the **Capstone Facilitator Gem** simulates this real-world friction. Mid-way through the challenge, the Gem will inject a surprise update from a related department (e.g., "Engineering just flagged a delay").

This forces participants to adapt their strategy on the fly using AI, simulating real corporate cross-functional collaboration.

## 🔀 NotebookLM Synergy (Multi-Department Trainings)

If you are training multiple related departments in the same session (e.g., Commercial and Projects & Engineering, or IT and Legal), the capstones are designed to intersect natively.

**Do not have them work together during the challenge.** They must solve their piece independently based on the Gem's data.

**The Synergy Reveal:** During the debrief, have both departments upload their final deliverables (reports, slider decks) into a single, shared NotebookLM. Ask NotebookLM: _"Analyze these proposals. Where do the departments' assumptions clash, and what hidden systemic risks are exposed by combining their views?"_ This creates a powerful "aha" moment about cross-functional data siloes.
