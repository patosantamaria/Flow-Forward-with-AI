## 1. Vision & Purpose

Our vision is to embed Artificial Intelligence into the core of our operations, workflow and decision-making processes, transforming the Vopak AME BU into a more efficient, innovative, and data-driven organization.

The purpose of this journey is to empower our AME BU colleagues by:

- Equipping them with practical skills to leverage Gemini and other basic AI tools to solve real-world business challenges.
- Exciting colleagues about the possibilities Gen AI can offer them instead of seeing it as a threat or something too complex.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

AME BU has an opportunity to enhance efficiency, innovation, and data-driven decision-making by adopting Artificial Intelligence tools. To achieve this, colleagues must be equipped with the necessary skills to use AI tools like Gemini to solve business challenges. This project provides a structured path to build the required skills, confidence, and a culture of continuous AI-driven improvement from the ground up.

Vopak Global are working on more complex use cases involving Agentic AI. At AME BU we aim to focus on basic competency and skills to complement global ambitions.

---

## 3. The AME BU AI Empowerment Journey: A 3-Phase Program

**Target Audience:** AME BU Staff
**Participants:** AME BU Colleagues across all functions (Operations & Safety, HR, Finance, Commercial, Communications)

|            | Phase 1             | Phase 2              | Phase 3          |
| :--------- | :------------------ | :------------------- | :--------------- |
| **Name**   | Foundation & Ethics | Core Tools Deep Dive | Automate & Build |
| **Timing** | October             | November             | December onwards |
| **Format** | Remote Online       | 1 Week On Site (SG)  | On-site Workshop |

### Phase 1: Foundation & Ethics (October 2025 — Remote, Online)

**Objective:** Ensure all AME BU colleagues understand fundamental AI concepts, are comfortable using core tools like Gemini in Workspace, and can identify immediate, low-effort opportunities for efficiency gains.

**Focus:** Demystifying AI and building baseline confidence and competence across all functions.

**Activities:**

- Patricio & Martijn host a kick-off webinar to explain the "Why" behind the initiative.
- Schedule 60-minute virtual introduction sessions tailored to each function (HR, Finance, Operations, etc.).
- Distribute a self-study curriculum using Google's Workspace training modules and custom Vopak "quick start" guides.
- Provide links to courses on Cloudskillsboost.
- Prepare and deploy a survey to gather potential use cases for Phase 2.

**Measured Outcome:**

- A broad base of colleagues are comfortable with the basic principles and application of generative AI within Google Workspace, Gems & NotebookLM.
- Small-scale efficiency gains and time savings are documented.
- A list of potential use cases is generated for exploration in Phase 2.

### Phase 2: Core Tools Deep Dive (November 2025 — AI Week in Singapore)

**Objective:** Generate excitement, foster collaboration within functions, and produce working AI-enabled solutions that address significant business challenges.

**Focus:** An intensive, 5-day in-person "AI Summit" in Singapore designed to convert foundational knowledge into tangible, business-relevant solutions.

**Activities:**

**Morning Session (General): Introduction to AI concepts and tools.**

- Overview of fundamental AI concepts (ML, Deep Learning, Generative AI).
- Vopak Policies regarding the safe, ethical and compliant use of AI.
- Practical introduction to readily available AI tools (Gemini suite & ChatwithVopak).
- Discuss responsible AI use and best practices.

**Afternoon Session (Focused): Use Case Building Workshop.**

- Guided brainstorming session for function-specific AI use cases.
- Structured framework for evaluating feasibility and potential business impact.
- Prototype a high-level plan for the top use cases.

**End of Day: Capstone Challenge.**

- Function teams work through a tailored Capstone challenge.
- Apply all the AI skills and tools learned together as a team.
- Present the outcome.

**Measured Outcome:**

- Awareness and understanding of Vopak AI policies.
- Gemini Workspace, Gems & NotebookLM trained BU staff.
- AI Champions per team are nominated.

### Phase 3: Automate & Build (December 2025 onwards)

**Objective:** Create a self-sustaining ecosystem of AI adoption, driven by internal champions and supported by a continuous learning culture.

**Focus:** Embedding new skills and solutions into the daily fabric of the organization to ensure long-term value creation.

**Activities:**

- Establish an AI Champions Program, where nominated champions from each function receive advanced training.
- Build a Community of Practice (CoP) through a weekly virtual "AI Hour" and a dedicated Google Chat space for real-time sharing.
- Begin a Structured Rollout to Terminals, led by AI Champions who will adapt successful use cases to the needs of each terminal.
- Implement Tracking & Governance via a dashboard to monitor key metrics and demonstrate ROI.

**Measured Outcome:**

- Basic AI proficiency becomes a widespread capability, not a niche skill.
- A continuous cycle of innovation is established, where new efficiencies are identified from the ground up.
- Measurable business impact is achieved through increased productivity, improved safety, cost savings and enhanced commercial effectiveness.

## 5. Success Metrics

### Adoption Metrics

- Number of active weekly Gemini users.
- Usage growth rate per function.

### Impact Metrics

- Number of AI-enabled workflows automated.
- Estimated hours saved per month.
- Reduction in errors/rework.
- Measurable impact on productivity, safety, and commercial effectiveness.

### Innovation Metrics

- Pipeline of new use cases submitted.
- Number of use cases successfully deployed post-AI Week.
