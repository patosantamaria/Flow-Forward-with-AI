# HR Capstone A — "The Project Banyan Expansion"

> **Origin:** Created by Yinxien & Leonard Leong (BU Singapore), enhanced by CoE with full tool coverage.
> **Best for:** BU Singapore or any BU where you can reference a real local terminal and local employment regulations.

---

## The Scenario

Vopak has just secured an **emergency 12-month contract** to manage a new high-priority chemical storage wing at a local terminal. This requires a dedicated "Quick Response Team" of **10 Terminal Operators** to be fully onboarded, costed, and briefed by the end of the week.

Your group must act as the **HR Task Force** to deliver a complete "Operational Readiness HR Package" using AI to bypass hours of manual work.

---

## 🎤 Final Deliverables — What You Leave With

> 💡 This capstone uses **every tool** you learned in training. You leave with: a **NotebookLM knowledge base**, a **Deep Research report**, a **written analysis with budget**, a **slide deck**, and optionally a **video**. Open your **Capstone Facilitator Gem** in a separate tab for prompt tips and guidance.

### Step 1: Build Your Knowledge Base — NotebookLM (10 min)

Upload all documents from the Data Room into a **NotebookLM** notebook. This becomes your grounded, source-cited research space.

> **Try:** "Across all uploaded documents, what are the regulatory requirements for shift workers handling hazardous chemicals? What are the maximum allowable overtime hours?"

> **Bonus:** Generate an **Audio Overview** of your notebook — listen to a 2-minute summary while you plan your approach.

### Step 2: Deep Research — Gemini Deep Research (10 min)

Use **Gemini's Deep Research** feature for the research phase of your challenge.

> 💡 **Tip:** Consider turning **off Google Search** if you want Deep Research to focus exclusively on your uploaded Drive documents and Data Room. Turn it **on** if you want it to also pull in external best practices (e.g., market salary benchmarks, industry onboarding frameworks).

**Research prompt (with web):**

> "Research the current regulatory requirements for hazardous chemical terminal operators in [Singapore/your region]. Include: mandatory certifications, maximum overtime allowances for 12-hour shift workers, market salary benchmarks for terminal operators in the chemical storage sector, and best practices for rapid onboarding of technical staff."

**Research prompt (Drive-only):**

> "Based on the documents in my Drive, synthesize: the Employee Handbook requirements for shift allowances, overtime limits, and onboarding procedures. Cross-reference against the regulatory requirements. Identify any compliance gaps."

> **How:** Open Gemini Web App → type your prompt → click the **Deep Research** button → review the research plan → let it run → review the full report with citations.

### Step 3: Analysis & Drafting — Gemini Web App + Workspace (20 min)

Three tasks, one per HR sub-function:

#### Task A: The Recruitment Sprint (Recruitment & HRBP)

> **The Problem:** You need a specialised Job Description and a technical interview guide for "Hazardous Chemical Operators."

> **Prompt:** "Act as a Vopak HR Business Partner. Draft a Job Description for 'Hazardous Chemical Terminal Operator' that includes: (1) Vopak-specific safety values (Fundamentals on Safety), (2) local regulatory requirements for chemical handling [e.g., Singapore MOM Workplace Safety & Health Act], (3) required certifications, (4) 4-3-3-4 shift rotation details. Then generate a 5-question behavioural interview rubric designed to test 'Safety under Pressure' — for each question include: the competency assessed, a sample strong answer, and a scoring guide (1-5)."

> **Deliverable:** A polished JD and Interview Scorecard in **Google Docs**.

#### Task B: The Policy & Compliance Check (Comp & Ben / Payroll)

> **The Problem:** The new team works a "4-3-3-4" shift rotation. You need to verify shift allowance and overtime calculations are 100% compliant with the Employee Handbook and local employment law.

> **Prompt (in NotebookLM):** "According to the uploaded documents: (1) What is the maximum allowable overtime for a 12-hour shift worker? (2) What shift allowance percentage applies? (3) Are there any restrictions on consecutive night shifts?"

> **Prompt (in Gemini Sheets):** "Calculate the pro-rated monthly cost for a team of 10 Terminal Operators with a base salary of $3,500 + 15% shift allowance + overtime cap. Include columns: Component | Per Person | Team of 10 | Monthly | Annual. Add a row for total employer cost including CPF/social contributions."

> **Deliverable:** A compliance risk summary and a high-level budget table in **Google Sheets**.

#### Task C: The Employee Communication (HRBP)

> **The Problem:** Current terminal staff are worried this new project will increase their workload.

> **Prompt:** "Act as the HR Director. Draft an internal announcement email that: (1) explains the new terminal expansion and what it means for the team, (2) emphasises that this creates career growth opportunities (not extra burden), (3) addresses workload concerns directly ('dedicated team, not additional workload for you'), (4) mentions training and support available. Tone: supportive, transparent, confident. Vopak voice."

> **Deliverable:** A ready-to-send announcement email in **Gmail** (via side panel).

### Step 4: Executive Slide Deck — Google Slides (10 min)

Create a **5-slide "HR Operational Readiness Package"** for the BU Director.

> **How:** Take your Google Doc deliverables → open **Google Slides** → use "Help me visualise" or the Gemini side panel to generate slides from your content.
>
> **Slide structure:**
>
> 1. Title — "Project [Terminal] Expansion: HR Readiness Package"
> 2. Recruitment Plan — JD summary, timeline, interview process
> 3. Budget & Compliance — Cost table, regulatory compliance status
> 4. Communication Strategy — Announcement approach, key messages
> 5. Timeline & Next Steps — Week 1-4 onboarding milestones

> **Alternative:** Use the **Slide Deck Creator Gem** → paste your key findings → copy structured content into Google Slides.

This is your **required** deliverable — you present this in 2 minutes.

### Step 5 (Optional Bonus): Narrated Video — Google Vids (10 min)

Create a short onboarding welcome video for the new Terminal Operators.

1. Open [Google Vids](https://vids.google.com)
2. Click **Create** → import your slide deck
3. Let Vids auto-generate a narration script
4. Adjust tone and pacing — make it welcoming
5. Generate and preview (keep under 2 minutes)

> **Why:** A polished welcome video for new hires makes a powerful first impression and shows HR innovation.

> **Facilitator:** Create these documents in Google Docs/Sheets **before** the session and place them in a shared folder.

| :-- | :----------------------------------------------- | :----------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **Employee Handbook (HR & Shift Work Sections)** | Google Doc   | Shift allowance policy, overtime limits, onboarding requirements, probation terms.                                                             |
| 2   | **Local Employment Regulations Summary**         | Google Doc   | Key clauses from employment law relevant to shift workers: overtime caps, rest day requirements, night shift restrictions, mandatory benefits. |
| 3   | **AI Acceptable Use Policy**                     | Google Doc   | Vopak's policy on AI usage in HR processes.                                                                                                    |
| 4   | **Terminal Safety Requirements**                 | Google Doc   | PPE requirements, mandatory certifications for chemical handling, safety induction programme outline.                                          |
