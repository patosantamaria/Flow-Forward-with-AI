# HR Capstone B — "Project Ascend"

> **Origin:** Flow Forward CoE. Generic version — works for any BU without modification.
> **Best for:** Any BU where you want a strategic, forward-looking HR challenge focused on AI reskilling and ethical AI governance.

---

## The Scenario

The CEO has mandated a rapid AI integration across all business functions. The HR team must design the **workforce reskilling strategy** while ensuring **ethical AI guardrails** for recruitment and performance management. The Works Council is watching — any misstep in employee rights will trigger a formal objection.

HR needs a reskilling plan, ethical AI guidelines, and a board-ready presentation **by the end of this session**.

---

## 🎤 Final Deliverables — What You Leave With

> 💡 This capstone uses **every tool** you learned in training. You leave with: a **NotebookLM knowledge base**, a **Deep Research report**, a **written analysis**, a **slide deck**, and optionally a **video**. Open your **Capstone Facilitator Gem** in a separate tab for prompt tips and guidance.

### Step 1: Build Your Knowledge Base — NotebookLM (10 min)

Upload all documents from the Data Room into a **NotebookLM** notebook. This becomes your grounded, source-cited research space.

> **Try:** "Across all uploaded documents, what are the 3 most critical issues I should focus on for an AI reskilling programme?"

> **Bonus:** Generate an **Audio Overview** of your notebook — listen to a 2-minute summary while you plan your approach.

### Step 2: Deep Research — Gemini Deep Research (15 min)

Use **Gemini's Deep Research** feature to build a comprehensive research foundation.

> 💡 **Tip:** Consider turning **off Google Search** if you want Deep Research to focus exclusively on your uploaded Drive documents and Data Room. Turn it **on** if you want external research (industry case studies, competitor programmes, academic best practices).

**Research prompt (with web):**

> "Research best practices for AI reskilling programmes in industrial organisations. Identify: (1) the most effective training formats for non-technical staff, (2) common resistance points and how to address them, (3) metrics to measure reskilling success, (4) examples from the energy, chemicals, or infrastructure sector. Present as a structured brief with source citations."

**Research prompt (Drive-only):**

> "Based on the documents in my Drive, synthesize: the current L&D policies, Works Council requirements for technology introduction, and any existing AI usage guidelines. Identify gaps that need to be addressed in a reskilling plan."

> **How:** Open Gemini Web App → type your prompt → click the **Deep Research** button → review the research plan → let it run → review the full report.

### Step 3: Analysis & Drafting — Gemini Web App + Workspace (30 min)

Two core deliverables:

#### Deliverable 1: 6-Month Workforce Reskilling Plan

> **Prompt:** "Draft a 6-month Workforce Reskilling & Transition Plan for Vopak. Structure:
>
> - **Month 1-2 (Awareness):** Leadership alignment, baseline skills assessment, communication campaign
> - **Month 3-4 (Skills Building):** Phased training rollout, hands-on workshops, online modules, mentoring pairs
> - **Month 5-6 (Integration):** AI embedded in daily workflows, AI Champions programme, ROI measurement
>
> For each phase: target audience, activities, resources needed, success metrics. Include a specific pathway for AI Champions development."

> Refine in **Google Docs** using the side panel: "Make this more actionable. Add specific KPIs for each phase."

#### Deliverable 2: Ethical AI Guidelines for Recruitment & Performance

> **Prompt:** "Draft Ethical Guidelines for AI in Recruitment & Performance Management at Vopak. Cover:
>
> 1. **Recruitment:** What AI can and cannot be used for in hiring decisions (screening OK, final decisions never)
> 2. **Performance Management:** Transparency requirements when AI is used in reviews
> 3. **Data Privacy:** Protections for employee data — no PII in AI tools, anonymisation requirements
> 4. **Works Council:** Notification and consultation requirements before implementing AI in HR processes
> 5. **Accountability:** Who is responsible when AI-assisted decisions are challenged?
>
> Tone: policy-grade, clear, enforceable."

> **Gemini in Sheets** _(if applicable)_: Create a skills assessment matrix — Department | Role | Current AI Level | Target Level | Gap | Recommended Path.

### Step 4: Executive Slide Deck — Google Slides (15 min)

Create a **5-7 slide executive presentation** for the board.

> **How:** Take your Google Doc deliverables → open **Google Slides** → use "Help me visualise" or the Gemini side panel to generate slides.
>
> **Slide structure:**
>
> 1. Title — "Project Ascend: Workforce AI Reskilling Strategy"
> 2. Why Now — Strategic context (Shifting Gears 2030, competitive pressure)
> 3. The Plan — 6-month roadmap visual (Awareness → Skills → Integration)
> 4. Key Numbers — Skills gap data, training reach, projected time savings
> 5. Ethical Guardrails — 3-bullet summary of AI in HR guidelines
> 6. Investment & ROI — Budget, expected productivity gains
> 7. Next Steps & Ask — Board decision required

> **Alternative:** Use the **Vopak Slide Designer** → paste findings → copy into Google Slides.

This is your **required** deliverable — you present this in 2 minutes.

### Step 5 (Optional Bonus): Narrated Video — Google Vids (10 min)

Turn your slide deck into a narrated CEO communication video.

1. Open [Google Vids](https://vids.google.com)
2. Click **Create** → import your Google Slides deck
3. Let Vids auto-generate a narration script
4. Adjust tone — make it inspiring, change-management style
5. Generate and preview (keep under 3 minutes)

> **Why:** A polished video is the perfect format for the CEO's company-wide AI transformation announcement.
