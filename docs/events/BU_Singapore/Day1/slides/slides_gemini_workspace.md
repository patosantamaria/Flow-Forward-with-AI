### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini in Workspace — Your Side Panel Assistant"
- Subtitle: "AI built into Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides — where you already work"

### Slide 2: What Is the Side Panel?

- The Gemini icon in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides
- One click opens your AI assistant — no switching apps
- It reads the document/email you're looking at and works with it
- Key rule: "If it's in your Drive, Gemini can read it. Use `@` to reference files."

### Slide 3: Demo — Gmail (HR Email Triage)

- Scenario: Long email thread about a new hire onboarding issue
- Prompt shown: "Summarize this email thread. What are the 3 key decisions made, what's still pending, and who owes an action item?"
- Result: Structured summary with action items attributed to owners
- HR use case: Policy question response, interview scheduling chains, benefits inquiries

### Slide 4: Demo — Docs (Onboarding Checklist)

- Scenario: Drafting a 30-day onboarding checklist for a new Terminal Operations Engineer
- Prompt shown: "Act as a Vopak HR Business Partner. Draft a 30-day onboarding checklist for a new Terminal Operations Engineer joining Singapore. Include IT setup, SHEQ induction, system access, buddy programme. Columns: Week, Activity, Owner, Prerequisites, Completion Criteria."
- The `@` connector: referencing existing Drive documents for context

### Slide 5: Demo — Sheets (Learning Path Design)

- Scenario: Designing a 6-month learning path with AI literacy milestones
- Prompt shown: "Design a 6-month learning path for a new engineer. Weekly plan with columns: Week, Topic, Format, Resource, Milestone."
- Result: Structured table with AI milestones at Month 2, 4, and 6

### Slide 6: Your Turn — Practice Exercises

- Exercise 1: Onboarding Journey Design (Docs) — 10 min
- Exercise 2: Employee Communication — AI Champions Network (Gmail) — 10 min
- Exercise 3: Learning Path Design (Sheets) — 10 min
- "Use the exact prompts on the handout. Adapt them to your role."

### Slide 7: Key Takeaway

- "The Side Panel is your first touchpoint. Before opening a new tab, try the Gemini icon."
- "Gmail: Summarize, draft replies. Docs: Draft, refine, compare. Sheets: Analyse, visualise."
