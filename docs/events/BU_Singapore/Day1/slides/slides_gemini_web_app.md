# Day 1 — Gemini Web App: Slide Deck Outline

## Audience: HR + Office Management + DSAM VAS (9 pax)

> **Use this outline with the Vopak Slide Designer to generate a tailored slide deck for the Gemini Web App module.**

---

### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini Web App — Heavy Lifting"
- Subtitle: "Upload documents, do deep analysis, research the web, and create presentations"

### Slide 2: When to Use the Web App vs. the Side Panel

- Side Panel: Quick tasks on the document you're already reading
- Web App: Complex analysis, multi-document comparison, deep research, long-form drafting
- Rule of thumb: "If you're uploading files or need more than 2 minutes of AI thinking — use the Web App"

### Slide 3: Demo — Document Comparison (HR Policy Review)

- Scenario: Two versions of the Employee Handbook L&D section — what changed?
- Prompt shown: "Compare these two versions. List every change, categorise as New Policy, Modified Policy, or Removed Policy. Flag changes requiring Works Council consultation."
- Result: Structured comparison table with Works Council flags
- HR impact: Policy audits, handbook updates, regulatory change tracking

### Slide 4: Demo — Deep Research

- Scenario: Researching best practices for AI reskilling in industrial organisations
- Prompt shown: "Research best practices for AI reskilling programs in industrial organisations. Identify effective training formats, common resistance points, and success metrics."
- How it works: Deep Research creates a multi-source report with citations
- How to activate: Click the Deep Research button or select from model picker
- Tip: Turn off Google Search to focus research on your Drive documents only

### Slide 5: Time Saved Exercise

- Fill in the table: My Repetitive Task | How Often? | Time per Occurrence | AI Alternative | Time with AI
- Example rows for HR: writing onboarding emails (30 min → 3 min), answering policy questions (15 min → 2 min), drafting JDs (1 hour → 10 min)
- "Calculate your total monthly time savings. Share with the room."
