## The Mandate

As part of our **Shifting Gears 2030** strategy, I am mandating the rapid integration of AI-assisted workflows across all Vopak business functions. This is not optional — it is a strategic imperative.

Our competitors are already deploying AI for predictive maintenance, automated reporting, and customer intelligence. We must move faster.

## What I Need from HR

I am asking every BU HR team to develop two things within the next 30 days:

### 1. A 6-Month Workforce Reskilling & Transition Plan

- **Who** needs to be trained (every knowledge worker, prioritised by department)
- **What** skills they need (AI literacy, tool proficiency, prompt engineering, ethical AI awareness)
- **How** we deliver it (phased: awareness → skills building → integration)
- **How** we measure success (concrete KPIs, not vague sentiment surveys)
- **AI Champions pathway** — we need peer mentors in every department

### 2. Ethical AI Guidelines for Recruitment & Performance Management

The Works Council has raised concerns about AI being used in hiring and performance reviews. They are right to be cautious. I need clear guidelines that:

- Define what AI **can** and **cannot** be used for in recruitment (screening assistance: yes; final hiring decisions: never)
- Ensure **transparency** when AI influences performance evaluations
- Protect **employee data privacy** — no personal data in AI tools without proper safeguards
- Specify **Works Council notification requirements** before any AI-assisted HR process goes live
- Establish **accountability** — who is responsible when an AI-assisted decision is challenged?

## Non-Negotiables

1. **No employee will be made redundant because of AI.** This is a reskilling programme, not a headcount reduction exercise.
2. **Works Council engagement is mandatory.** Any AI deployment touching employee evaluation must go through formal consultation.
3. **Safety override.** In all safety-critical functions, human judgement overrides AI recommendations. Always.
