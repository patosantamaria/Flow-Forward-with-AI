# Day 5 — Gemini Web App: Slide Deck Outline

## Audience: Safety + Terminal Management + Customer Service + Engineering + HR (10 pax)

> **Use this outline with the Vopak Slide Designer to generate a tailored slide deck for the Gemini Web App module.**

---

### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini Web App — Heavy Lifting"
- Subtitle: "Upload documents, analyse images, do deep research, and create presentations"

### Slide 2: When to Use the Web App vs. the Side Panel

- Side Panel: Quick tasks on the document you're reading
- Web App: Complex analysis, image analysis, deep research, SOP review
- For operations: "Upload a terminal photo and get a safety analysis in seconds. Upload an SOP and find the gaps."

### Slide 3: Demo — Visual Analysis (The Showstopper 📸)

- Scenario: Upload a photo of a terminal loading bay or industrial site
- Prompt shown: "Analyse this image. Identify any visible safety concerns based on Vopak Fundamentals on Safety and ISGOTT standards. For each: describe the risk, classify severity, and recommend corrective action."
- Result: Structured safety observation with risk ratings
- Safety impact: "AI sees what you see — and flags what you might miss"
- This is the jaw-dropping moment for operational staff

### Slide 4: Demo — SOP Gap Analysis

- Scenario: Upload an existing SOP
- Prompt shown: "Review this SOP for completeness. Identify missing steps, ambiguous language, or safety gaps compared to ISGOTT best practices."
- Result: Gap report with specific improvement recommendations
- Operations impact: "SOP reviews that take a day → done in 10 minutes"

### Slide 5: Time Saved Exercise

- Fill in: My Repetitive Task | How Often? | Time per Occurrence | AI Alternative | Time with AI
- Example rows: Writing shift handovers (every shift, 30 min → 3 min), structuring incident reports (as needed, 4-6 hrs → 30 min), reviewing SOPs for updates (quarterly, 1 day → 1 hr)
- "Calculate your total. Multiply by every shift supervisor in the BU."

### Slide 6: The Vopak Slide Designer

- Turn your safety analysis or operational reports into presentations
- Open the Vopak Slide Designer → paste your findings → get structured Vopak-branded slide content
- Prompt tip: "Create a 5-slide presentation covering: [paste your safety analysis]. Include key findings, risk ratings, recommended actions, and timeline."
- Copy output into Google Slides and refine
- Ops use: safety briefings, toolbox talk slides, management safety reviews

### Slide 7: Key Takeaway

- "The Web App is your operations analyst: image analysis, SOP reviews, and incident report structuring."
- "The visual analysis capability is a game-changer for safety observations."
- "Use the Vopak Slide Designer to present safety findings in a clear, professional format."
