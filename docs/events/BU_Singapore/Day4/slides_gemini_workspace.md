### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini in Workspace — Your Side Panel Assistant"
- Subtitle: "AI built into Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides — where you already work"

### Slide 2: What Is the Side Panel?

- The Gemini icon in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides
- One click opens your AI assistant — reads the document you're looking at
- Key rule: "If it's in your Drive, Gemini can read it. Use `@` to reference files."

### Slide 3: Demo — Gmail (Customer Email Intelligence)

- Scenario: Long customer email thread about contract negotiation
- Prompt shown: "Summarize this customer email thread. What are they really asking for? What's their main concern? Draft a response that addresses their concern while protecting our position on pricing."
- Result: Email with customer intent analysis and strategic response
- Commercial impact: "Read between the lines of every email — instantly"

### Slide 4: Demo — Sheets (Commercial Master Plan Analysis)

- Scenario: PMC (Product Market Combination) data with utilization, expiry dates, and revenue
- Prompt shown: "Analyse this data: identify PMCs with highest utilization, agreements expiring in the next 12 months, and capacity gaps that represent growth opportunities."
- Result: Strategic overview with growth opportunities flagged
- Commercial impact: "Spot deal opportunities hiding in your own data"

### Slide 5: Demo — Docs (T&C Comparison)

- Scenario: Customer proposed terms vs. Vopak standard Terms & Conditions
- Prompt shown: "Compare our standard T&Cs with this customer's proposed terms. Identify all deviations. Classify each as Acceptable, Negotiate, or Reject."
- Result: Deviation table with risk classification
- The `@` connector: reference Vopak standard T&Cs from Drive

### Slide 6: Your Turn — Practice Exercises

- Exercise 1: Customer Communication SLE-Aligned (Gmail) — Draft a quarterly business review email — 10 min
- Exercise 2: RFP Clause Analysis (Docs) — Identify non-standard clauses in a customer contract — 10 min
- Exercise 3: Competitive Research (Web App) — Compare Vopak vs. competitors in Asia-Pacific — 10 min
- "Frame every exercise as a deal you need to close. Speed matters."

### Slide 7: Key Takeaway

- "For Commercial: Gmail = customer intelligence. Sheets = deal pipeline insights. Docs = contract defence."
- "The Side Panel gives you the first-mover advantage in every deal."
