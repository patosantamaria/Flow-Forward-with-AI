## Section 3: Green Fuel Market Intelligence — Singapore

### Singapore Maritime Green Fuel Landscape

- **MPA Green Bunkering Initiatives:** Singapore MPA launched the Maritime Singapore Green Initiative (MSGI) to promote Green Bunkering. Green Methanol is a priority fuel alongside Green Ammonia.
- **IMO 2030 Targets:** 40% reduction in carbon intensity by 2030 vs. 2008 baseline. Green Methanol is one of the leading candidates for compliance.
- **Singapore Energy 2050:** Singapore targets net-zero by 2050. Maritime decarbonisation is a key pillar.
- **Bunkering Volume:** Singapore is the world's largest bunkering hub (~50 million tonnes/year). Green Fuel bunkering is projected to reach 5–8% of total volume by 2028.
- **Regulatory Support:** PFA (Port Facility Agreement) framework for Green Methanol bunkering — Vopak Singapore can apply.

### Key Data Points for Pitch

- Singapore handled 4.2 million tonnes of Green Fuel bunker enquiries in 2025 (MPA data)
- 15 major shipping lines have committed to Green Methanol by 2028
- Vopak's Fundamentals on Safety programme is globally recognised
- Jurong Island's strategic location at the intersection of major east-west shipping routes
