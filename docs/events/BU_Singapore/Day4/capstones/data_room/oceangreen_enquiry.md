### 5. Commercial Terms

#### 5.1 Pricing

Competitive storage tariff expected. OceanGreen will benchmark against at least 2 alternative Jurong Island operators.

#### 5.2 Most Favoured Customer

> **Clause 5.2:** OceanGreen requires "Most Favoured Customer" status. Any storage rate reduction, volume discount, or commercial incentive offered to any other customer at the same terminal must be **automatically extended** to OceanGreen for the duration of this contract.

#### 5.3 Payment Terms

Standard 30-day payment terms from invoice date.

#### 5.4 Liability & Indemnification

> **Clause 5.4:** The terminal operator (Vopak) shall be solely responsible for any product contamination, quality degradation, or loss occurring between custody transfer at the ship manifold and delivery to the bunkering vessel. **Vopak's liability shall not be capped** and shall include all consequential damages including but not limited to vessel delay costs, engine damage claims from end-users, and reputational harm to OceanGreen Maritime's brand.

#### 5.5 Exclusivity

> **Clause 5.5:** During the 4-hour bunkering windows allocated to OceanGreen, the assigned jetty and all associated transfer lines shall be **exclusively reserved** for OceanGreen operations. No other vessel or barge may occupy the berth or use the transfer infrastructure during these windows. OceanGreen reserves the right to request **additional exclusive windows** with 48 hours' notice, which Vopak shall not unreasonably refuse.

> ⚠️ **FACILITATOR NOTE — DO NOT SHARE WITH PARTICIPANTS:**
>
> **The 3 hidden traps:**
>
> 1. **Clause 5.2 — Most Favoured Customer:** This would force Vopak to match ANY discount given to ANY other customer. It would destroy pricing flexibility and margin management. Counter: offer competitive pricing with an annual review mechanism, but reject automatic parity.
> 2. **Clause 5.4 — Unlimited Liability:** Standard Vopak position caps liability at 100% of the value of product in the tank at the time of the incident. Unlimited liability + consequential damages is commercially unacceptable. Counter: cap liability at 150% of product value, exclude consequential damages, require mutual insurance.
> 3. **Clause 5.5 — Exclusive Berthing:** Guaranteed exclusive windows with the right to request additional windows "not unreasonably refused" would effectively give OceanGreen veto power over Vopak's jetty scheduling. Counter: guaranteed windows within agreed schedule, additional windows subject to availability on first-come-first-served basis.
