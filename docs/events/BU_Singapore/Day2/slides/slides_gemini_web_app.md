# Day 2 — Gemini Web App: Slide Deck Outline

## Audience: IT/Digital + Engineering + Ops + HR (11 pax)

> **Use this outline with the Vopak Slide Designer to generate a tailored slide deck for the Gemini Web App module.**

---

### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini Web App — Heavy Lifting"
- Subtitle: "Upload documents, run deep analysis, research, and create presentations"

### Slide 2: When to Use the Web App vs. the Side Panel

- Side Panel: Quick tasks on the document you're reading
- Web App: Complex analysis, multi-document uploads, deep research, code review
- For this technical group: "The Web App is your analysis engine — SoW stress tests, risk assessments, regulatory research"

### Slide 3: Demo — SoW Deep Analysis

- Scenario: Upload a vendor Statement of Work for a DCS upgrade
- Prompt shown: "Act as a Senior Vopak Project Engineer with 15 years of terminal operations experience. Review this SoW for a DCS upgrade and identify: (1) vague deliverables lacking acceptance criteria, (2) scope gaps — what's missing, (3) risk areas — integration points, dependencies, single points of failure, (4) clauses that deviate from our standard project governance."
- Result: Structured analysis with risk ratings
- Engineering impact: Normally takes 2-3 hours of careful reading → done in 5 minutes

### Slide 4: Demo — Deep Research

- Scenario: Researching best practices for terminal automation or cybersecurity standards
- Prompt shown: "Research current best practices for OT cybersecurity in chemical storage terminals. Include IEC 62443, NIST frameworks, and recent regulatory requirements in Asia-Pacific."
- How it works: Deep Research builds a multi-source report with citations
- How to activate: Click the Deep Research button or select from model picker
- Tip: Turn off Google Search to focus research on your uploaded Drive documents only

### Slide 5: Time Saved Exercise

- Fill in: My Repetitive Task | How Often? | Time per Occurrence | AI Alternative | Time with AI
- Example rows: SoW first-pass review (2 hrs → 10 min), writing risk assessments (3 hrs → 20 min), technical email drafting (30 min → 5 min), research for project proposals (4 hrs → 15 min)
- "Calculate your total monthly time savings. Share with the room."

### Slide 6: The Vopak Slide Designer

- Turn your analysis and reports into professional presentations
- Open the Vopak Slide Designer → paste your findings → get structured slide content in Vopak format
- Prompt tip: "Create a 5-7 slide presentation covering: [paste your SoW analysis]. Include title, key risks, recommendations, timeline, and next steps."
- Copy output into Google Slides and refine
- Engineering use: project proposals, risk assessment presentations, CAPEX justification decks

### Slide 7: Key Takeaway

- "The Web App handles what the Side Panel can't: multi-document analysis, deep research, and complex reasoning."
- "For Engineering: SoW stress tests, risk assessments, and regulatory research — all in minutes."
- "Use the Vopak Slide Designer to present your findings in a board-ready format."
