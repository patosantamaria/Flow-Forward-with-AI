### 🧠 09:55 — M4: PTCF Prompting Framework (25 min)

**Tailored PTCF Demo for Finance:**

| Element     | Concept         | Finance Example                                                                | Procurement Example                           |
| :---------- | :-------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Persona** | Give AI a role  | "Act as a Vopak Treasury Analyst"                                              | "Act as a Vopak Financial Controller"         |
| **Task**    | Specific action | "Compare these two CFF submissions"                                            | "Analyze this PO log and flag non-compliance" |
| **Context** | Background      | "Two 12-month CFFs from different BUs"                                         | "200+ open POs, some past 60 days"            |
| **Format**  | Output format   | "Reconciliation report: Line Item, BU1 Value, BU2 Value, Variance, Issue Type" | "Compliance table with recommended actions"   |

**The "Bad Briefing" Game:**

Say:

> "Compare these budgets."

Wait for the room to ask: "Which budgets? What period? What kind of comparison?"

> "You just wrote a prompt. Congratulations."

**Live PTCF comparison:**

**Bad prompt:**

> "Summarize this financial data."

**PTCF prompt:**

> "**[Persona]** Act as a Vopak Treasury Analyst responsible for BU Singapore CFF consolidation.
> **[Task]** Compare these two 12-month CFF submissions from different Business Units.
> **[Context]** We are in the month-end close. These two BUs have historically had naming inconsistencies and formatting differences that break the consolidation template.
> **[Format]** Present as a reconciliation report with columns: Line Item | BU1 Value | BU2 Value | Variance | Issue Type (naming, formatting, or numerical). Flag any variance above 5% in red."
