### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini Web App — Heavy Lifting"
- Subtitle: "Upload documents, run deep analysis, research, and create presentations"

### Slide 2: When to Use the Web App vs. the Side Panel

- Side Panel: Quick tasks on the document you're reading
- Web App: Complex analysis, multi-document comparison, deep research, long-form drafting
- For Finance: "Upload a full CAPEX proposal and a regulatory filing — get a cross-referenced analysis in minutes"

### Slide 3: Demo — CAPEX Proposal Analysis

- Scenario: Upload a CAPEX proposal with financial projections and strategic justification
- Prompt shown: "Act as a Vopak Financial Controller. Review this CAPEX proposal. Evaluate: NPV assumptions, sensitivity to key variables, alignment with Shifting Gears strategic priorities, and risk factors. Present as an Investment Committee brief."
- Result: Structured analysis with financial metrics and strategic alignment assessment
- Finance impact: "Board-ready analysis of a CAPEX proposal — 10 minutes vs. half a day"

### Slide 4: Demo — Deep Research

- Scenario: Researching regulatory changes or market benchmarks for financial planning
- Prompt shown: "Research current IFRS developments affecting lease accounting for industrial terminals. Summarize key changes, compliance timeline, and impact on Vopak's financial reporting."
- How it works: Deep Research builds a multi-source report with citations
- How to activate: Click the Deep Research button or select from model picker
- Tip: Turn off Google Search to focus on uploaded Drive documents only
