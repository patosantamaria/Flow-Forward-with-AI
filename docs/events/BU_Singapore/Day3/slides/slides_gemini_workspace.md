### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "Gemini in Workspace — Your Side Panel Assistant"
- Subtitle: "AI built into Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides — where you already work"

### Slide 2: What Is the Side Panel?

- The Gemini icon in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides
- One click opens your AI assistant — reads the document you're looking at
- Key rule: "If it's in your Drive, Gemini can read it. Use `@` to reference files."

### Slide 3: Demo — Gmail (Finance Communication)

- Scenario: Email thread about quarterly budget approval with multiple stakeholders
- Prompt shown: "Summarize this email thread. What's the budget status? What approvals are pending? List action items by person."
- Result: Structured summary with flagged pending approvals
- Finance use case: audit queries, vendor payment discussions, budget approval chains

### Slide 4: Demo — Sheets (Financial Data Analysis)

- Scenario: CFF reconciliation spreadsheet or budget tracker
- Prompt shown: "Act as a Vopak Financial Controller. Analyse this budget data: identify variances greater than 10%, explain likely causes for each variance, and flag any line items that need management attention."
- Show Gemini identifying patterns and generating a variance report
- Finance impact: "Your monthly variance analysis — first pass in 3 minutes"

### Slide 5: Demo — Docs (Procurement Contract Review)

- Scenario: Vendor contract or purchase order terms
- Prompt shown: "Act as a Vopak Procurement Specialist. Review this vendor contract. Identify: payment terms that deviate from our standard 30-day net, unusual liability clauses, and auto-renewal provisions."
- The `@` connector: reference Vopak procurement guidelines from Drive
