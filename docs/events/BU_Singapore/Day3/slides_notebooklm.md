### Slide 1: Module Title

- Title: "NotebookLM — Your Team's Brain"
- Subtitle: "Upload your documents. Ask questions. Get source-cited answers."

### Slide 2: What Makes NotebookLM Different

- Other AI tools guess. NotebookLM quotes.
- Every answer includes source citations — exact paragraphs and page numbers
- Your documents stay private — only you access your notebook
- For Finance: upload IFRS standards, policies, audit guidelines — get cited compliance answers

### Slide 3: How It Works

- Step 1: Create a notebook
- Step 2: Upload sources (Google Docs, PDFs, Drive files, websites)
- Step 3: Ask questions — get grounded, cited answers
- Key visual: Upload → Ask → Get cited answer with source highlight

### Slide 4: Demo — Financial Policy Q&A

- Upload: Treasury policy + Delegation of Authority matrix + CAPEX guidelines

- Result: Cited answer with exact policy references
- Finance impact: "Your compliance expert. 10-second lookups with exact clause references."

### Slide 5: Demo — Cross-Document Reconciliation

- Upload: Budget document + Actuals report + Forecast
- Prompt shown: "Compare budget vs. actuals vs. forecast. Identify the top 5 variances and cite which document contains each figure."
- Show the cross-referencing capability with multiple source citations
