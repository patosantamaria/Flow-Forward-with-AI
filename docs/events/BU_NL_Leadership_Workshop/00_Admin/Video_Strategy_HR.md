### Option A: "Welcome to the Gemini Era" (1:00) - **RECOMMENDED**

### Option B: "Gemini Speech-to-Speech Translation" (2:50)

**[Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xdPIwgDriTg)**

- **Why it's good:**
  - **Tangible:** Shows a very specific, magical capability.
