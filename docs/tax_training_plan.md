# Tailored Training Plan: Global Tax Team (Lead: Shirley Terhaar)

**Objective**: Move the Tax Team from "Manual consolidators" to "Strategic Architects" using Gemini.
**Key Driver**: Solving the 9 inefficiencies identified in the meeting.

## Phase 1: The "Quick Wins" Workshop (Half-Day)

_Focus: Immediate time savings on low-risk tasks._

### Module 1: The "CFO Briefer" (Activity: CFO Report)

- **Pain Point**: Manually reading IBFD newsletters/Pillar 2 updates.
- **Gemini Skill**: Summarization & Synthesis.
- **Exercise**:
  - _Input_: Upload 3 PDF Newsletters + Last Quarter's Report.
  - _Prompt_: "Based on the style of [Last Report], summarize the key regulatory changes from [Newsletters] for the 'Global Developments' slide."

### Module 2: The "Memo Simplifier" (Activity: WHT Compliance)

- **Pain Point**: Complex advisor memos (Brazilian Gross-up, Chinese VAT).
- **Gemini Skill**: Rewriting for Audience.
- **Exercise**:
  - _Input_: Complex Technical Memo.
  - _Prompt_: "Rewrite this technical advice as a checklist for a Junior Controller. No jargon, just actions."

## Phase 2: The "Deep Dive" Build-a-thon (Full Day)

_Focus: Building Custom Gems for complex workflows._

### BUILD 1: The "Transfer Pricing Architect" Gem

- **Goal**: Draft 80% of the Master File automatically.
- **System Instruction**: "You are Vopak's TP Expert. Use the Annual Report (uploaded) to draft the 'Business Overview' section of the Master File. Cross-reference with last year's TP Planning Report."
- **Success Metric**: Time to draft reduced from 3 days to 4 hours.

### BUILD 2: The "Non-Deductible Detective" Gem

- **Goal**: Sanity check GL ledgers for tax risks.
- **System Instruction**: "Analyze this GL dump. Flag any rows with 'Entertainment', 'Gifts', or 'Fines'. Categorize them as 'Likely Non-Deductible' or 'Requires Review'."

## Phase 3: Operations & Scale

- **Champions**: Nominate 1 "Tax Technologist" from Shirley's team to maintain the Gems.
- **Integration**: Add the _Transfer Pricing Architect_ to the Gemini CoE Portal under "Tax Gems".
