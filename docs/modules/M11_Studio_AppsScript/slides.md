## Slide Notes

- **Slides 2 & 3 (CAN/CANNOT):** This is the most common source of disappointment in M11. Set expectations early and clearly. Studios personal constraint is a security feature — frame it positively.
- **Slide 7:** If possible, demo Gemini writing an Apps Script LIVE. Open a new Apps Script project, paste Gemini's output, hit Run. The reaction when it works is one of the strongest moments in the programme.
- **Slide 8 IT Visibility:** Tone matters — this is not a compliance lecture. "One message, one log entry" is the message. Keep it light and fast.
- **Practice Level guidance:** Most participants will do Level 1 (Studio). Encourage one or two IT-literate participants in the room to try Level 2 — their success motivates others.
- **Closing note:** At the end of M11, participants have completed the full programme. Acknowledge this explicitly (Slide 10). It's a milestone worth celebrating.
