## Slide Notes

- **Slide 2:** Do NOT show an AI demo yet — this is conceptual. Keep it fast and punchy.
- **Slide 4:** If attendees have phones, ask them to open `gemini.google.com` right now with their Vopak account. First-login surprise moment.
- **Slide 5:** The "Smart Intern" analogy is the most important concept of M1. Come back to it throughout the day.
