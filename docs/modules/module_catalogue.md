## Module Quick Reference Cards

> Use these cards when designing a custom session. Each card shows the minimum viable module — what you need to run it standalone.

---

### M1 — GenAI Foundations `15 min · No prerequisites`

**What it covers:** The AI evolution (rule-based → ML → GenAI), what Generative AI creates, what Gemini is, Vopak's 4 tools, Enterprise Data Protection.

**What they leave with:** A shared vocabulary. Everyone understands what kind of engine is under the hood.

**Based on:** First 15 minutes of the _Gemini Introductory_ internal training by Patricio Santamaria.

**Can stand alone:** ✅ Yes — works as an opener for any session.

📄 [Full content → Phase 1 curriculum](phases/phase1_foundation_ethics.md#-module-1-genai-foundations--what-is-this-thing-15-min)

---

### M2 — The Flow Forward Vision `10 min · No prerequisites`

**What it covers:** Real Vopak friction points (Operations handover, Treasury CFF), career superpower framing, strategic context (Shifting Gears 2030, Project Ascend 292% ROI).

**What they leave with:** The "why." A personally relevant reason to invest in this.

**Can stand alone:** ✅ Yes — always pair with M1 for context.

📄 [Full content → Phase 1 curriculum](phases/phase1_foundation_ethics.md#-module-2-the-flow-forward-vision-10-min)

---

### M3 — Security & Zero Trust `20 min · Requires M1`

**What it covers:** No PII, no secrets, data cutoffs, hallucinations, financial/legal guardrails, Vopak's AI principles.

**What they leave with:** Clear rules of engagement — what they can and must never do with AI.

**Can stand alone:** ⚠️ Needs M1 for context on what Gemini actually is.

📄 [Full content → Phase 1 curriculum](phases/phase1_foundation_ethics.md#-module-3-security--guardrails-15-min)

### M4 — PTCF Prompting Framework `45 min (incl. practice) · Requires M1–M3`

**What it covers:** Starts with TCF (Task + Context + Format), then introduces Persona as the power upgrade via a live Vopak comparison exercise (same prompt, two personas — different output). Includes chain of thought and conditional logic concepts introduced ahead of M10.

**What they leave with:** A universal prompting framework they can apply immediately.

**Can stand alone:** ✅ Yes — the single most important module in the programme.

📄 [Full content → Phase 1 curriculum](phases/phase1_foundation_ethics.md#-module-4-the-tcf-prompting-framework-20-min)

### M5 — Live Demo: The Difference `15 min · Requires M4`

**What it covers:** Side-by-side comparison of a bad prompt vs. a PTCF-structured prompt. Departmental quick-fire.

**What they leave with:** Direct visual proof that better prompts produce better results.

**Can stand alone:** ⚠️ Only meaningful after M4.

📄 [Full content → Phase 1 curriculum](phases/phase1_foundation_ethics.md#-module-5-live-demo--the-difference-15-min)

**What they leave with:** Ability to triage emails, draft documents, and summarize meeting threads using the side panel — without copy-pasting.

**Can stand alone:** ✅ Yes — most immediately applicable module for daily users.

### M10 — Gems: Build Your AI Colleague `120 min · Requires M6–M9`

**What it covers:** Opens with advanced prompting techniques for Gem instruction writing (persona/role, chain of thought, conditional logic). Then: what Gems are and why they matter. Demo gallery (4 pre-built Gems). Guided build. Advanced Gems: grounding with files/NotebookLM. Solo build: your department Gem. Ends with **how to share Gems** — sharing via link, managing access, and promoting a Gem to a team-wide shared resource.

**What they leave with:** 2 working Gems — one guided, one of their own design.

> **Key framing:** "In Phase 2 you used AI. Today you build AI."

**Can stand alone:** ✅ Yes — excellent half-day workshop.

📄 [Full content → Phase 3 curriculum](phases/phase3_automate_build.md#-module-10-gems--build-your-ai-colleague)

### M11 — Workspace Studio & Apps Script `105 min · Requires M10`

**What it covers:**

- **Studio (60 min):** No-code AI flows — Starters, Steps, Variables. Email Triage flow live demo. Guided + solo flow build. Sharing and monitoring.
- **Apps Script (45 min):** The "last mile" bridge — Gemini writes the code, you click Run. 3-step pattern. Live demo using the Treasury automation example. Solo practice.

**What they leave with:** 1 Studio flow running on autopilot + 1 Gemini-written Apps Script ready to run.

> **Key framing for Apps Script:** "We are not learning to code. You describe the problem, Gemini writes the solution, you click Run."

**Can stand alone:** ✅ Yes — good afternoon follow-up to a morning Gems workshop (M10).

📄 [Full content → Phase 3 curriculum](phases/phase3_automate_build.md#-module-11-workspace-studio--apps-script)

## Delivery Principles

1. **Every module starts with a "Why this matters" hook.** Never open with features. Open with the problem it solves.
2. **Every module ends with a clear deliverable.** Participants leave with something they built or wrote.
3. **Practice is non-optional.** Every module with a practice component runs the practice — instruction without practice has near-zero retention.
4. **Modules can be skipped, but not abbreviated.** If you don't have time for M9, drop it. Don't cut it to 15 minutes.
5. **Always run M3 (Zero Trust) before any practice.** Never let participants experiment with real data without understanding the guardrails first.
