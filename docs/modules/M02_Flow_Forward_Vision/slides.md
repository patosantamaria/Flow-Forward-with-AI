## Slide Notes

- **Slide 1:** Use the Operations and Finance examples by default, then adapt to your audience — mention the BU or department in the room.
- **Slide 3:** These are real public numbers from Vopak's FY2025 Annual Report and Project Ascend. Use them confidently.
- **Energy check:** This module should feel energising and aspirational. If the room is quiet by the end of Slide 4, the pace was too slow.
