## Slide Notes

- **Slide 2:** "Cannot hallucinate about your files" is a slight simplification — it can still misinterpret content. The key point is it's grounded in your sources, not in its general training data. Clarify if asked.
- **Slide 5:** If you're running a SHEQ/Operations audience, use a PtW or emergency SOP. For Finance, use a CFF or Oracle approval guide.
- **Slide 6 Audio Overview:** If live audio is not possible (connectivity), show a pre-cued 60-second clip. The reaction is always worth it.
- **Slide 8 Practice:** Encourage participants to use real documents from their current projects. The more real the source, the more useful the practice.
