## Slide Notes

- **Slide 3 Gallery:** Always demo a Gem that is relevant to the audience department first. For SHEQ: Incident Investigator. For Finance: CFF Analyzer. For Operations: Shift Handover.
- **Slide 7 Live Build:** The facilitator building in real time — including making minor mistakes and fixing them — is more powerful than a polished demo. Authenticity builds confidence.
- **Slide 8 Build Sprint:** Have department Gem templates pre-loaded from `docs/departments/<dept>/04_Gem_Instructions.md` for participants who get stuck.
- **Slide 9 Pitches:** Allocate 2 min per person strictly. If group is larger than 8, select 4–5 volunteers.
- **IT Shadow IT note:** Remind participants that Gems with critical process knowledge should be flagged to IT for awareness (not permission — awareness). Prevents Shadow AI risk.
