## Slide Notes

- **Slide 2:** This is the highest surprise-factor demo in the whole programme. If participants have Android or iPhone, ask them to download the Gemini app before the session (pre-work note). Take the photo live during the session.
- **Slide 3 Camera Angle Guide:** For field staff photographing equipment tags or gauges — straight on, not at an angle; adequate ambient light; 20–40 cm distance. Include this tip card in the printed Quick-Start guide.
- **Optional add-on for Maintenance:** Show a photo of a valve nameplate or equipment tag → Gemini extracts all data fields for SAP entry.
- **If time is short:** Slides 2 and 3 can be combined into one live demo with two prompts (photo and voice).
