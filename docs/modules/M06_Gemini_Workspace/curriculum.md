---

## Learning Objective

Participants triage their inbox and draft a document from Drive using the Gemini side panel — and build the `@` mention habit that makes AI output actually reliable.

---

## 1.1 The Ultimate Triage Tool

The "Ask Gemini" side panel reads the active document or email _without_ you copy-pasting anything.

**Gmail Demo:**  
Open a CFF reconciliation email thread from Treasury. Prompt the side panel:

> "Summarize this thread. Identify which BU submissions are still outstanding and draft a reply that confirms the deadline but requests revised accrual figures from BU Brazil and BU NLA."

**Docs/Drive Demo:**  
Open a blank Doc. Prompt:

> "Draft an executive summary based on the @BU_NL_Q3_Financial_Review spreadsheet and @Stratcom_Meeting_Notes doc. Highlight any line items where OCR is trending below the 13% corporate target."

---

## 1.2 Using Google Workspace Connectors (`@`)

- Typing `@` summons files directly into the context window — no copy-paste needed.
- **The rule:** _"If it's in your Drive, Gemini can read it. If you don't `@` mention it, Gemini will guess. Never let it guess."_

---

## Cautions by Department

> **🔴 HR:** AI-drafted performance feedback must be 100% human-reviewed before it goes anywhere near an employee record. Use AI for structure and drafting only — the final judgment is always yours.

> **🔴 Customer Service:** When using AI to draft customer responses, add at least one personal detail or acknowledgement specific to that customer's situation. AI replies that feel canned damage the relationship. Always personalise.

---

## Practice 1: Side Panel Mastery (30 min)

Break into departments and execute specific prompts from department prompt cards.

> **Department-specific prompts are in:** `docs/departments/<department>/prompts.md`

1. **15 minutes:** Run 3 prompts from your department prompt card using your own inbox or Drive files.
2. **Debrief:** 2 people share their "Aha!" moment with the room.

**Cross-department rooms:** Assign prompt cards to tables regardless of who's sitting there — cross-function exposure produces the strongest insights.

---

## Key Takeaways

- @ your files. Always.
- The side panel is your co-pilot inside Workspace — it never leaves the app you're working in.
- Every department has prompts built for their exact role in `docs/departments/`.
