## Slide Notes

- **Slides 2 & 3:** Demo must be LIVE — open Gmail or Docs on your own Vopak account and show in real time.
- **Slide 5:** Deliver calmly and matter-of-factly — not alarmist. "Here's the rule" tone, not "here's a scary warning."
- **Practice (Slide 6):** Department prompt cards should be printed or sent via Chat before the session starts. Ref: `docs/departments/`.
- **Cross-department rooms:** Assign department prompt cards to tables randomly — intentional cross-pollination.
