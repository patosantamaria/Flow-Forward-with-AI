# Resistance Handling for Facilitators

The toughest moments in "Flow Forward with AI" aren't when a tool breaks — they're when a participant expresses fear, skepticism, or resistance to AI. Here is how to handle the 3 most common objections with empathy and evidence.

## 1. "This is going to replace my job"

**The Reality:** Gemini cannot do your job. It doesn't have your context, your relationships, or your judgement.
**What to say:** "AI won't replace you, but a colleague using AI might. The goal here is 'Skill Expansion' — we want to automate the boring 30% of your job so you can focus on the high-value 70% where your human judgement matters most."

## 2. "I don't trust the answers it gives me"

**The Reality:** The model hallucinates. Zero Trust is a feature, not a bug.
**What to say:** "You shouldn't trust it! That's why Module 3 focuses on Zero Trust. Treat Gemini like a very eager, very fast intern. It can draft the 80% version in seconds, but you must review and own the final 20%."

## 3. "I'm too busy to learn another new tool"

**The Reality:** Learning takes time, but the ROI is massive.
**What to say:** "I hear you. That's why we're focusing on tools you already use, like Gmail and Docs. If you spend 15 minutes learning a prompt today, it will save you 2 hours every week for the rest of the year."
