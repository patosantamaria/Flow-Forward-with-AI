# Vopak Corporate Slide Layouts (Template 2025)

> Reference for the `/create_slides` workflow. Use these layouts when planning slide structure.

## Layout Catalog

### 1. Cover Slide

- **Usage:** First slide only
- **Description:** Split design — deep blue left panel with title/date, photo right panel
- **Text Placement:** Title and date in bottom-left of the blue panel
- **Rule:** Subtitle MUST be today's date only. No additional subtitle text.

### 2. Table of Contents

- **Usage:** Main agenda/contents slide
- **Text Placement:** Right 50% — format the agenda list strictly in the right column
- **Description:** Deep blue left panel, white right panel with numbered list

### 3. Section Header

- **Usage:** MANDATORY before every new topic or major transition
- **Description:** Blank canvas, deep blue background, large centered title text
- **Rule:** Must be used liberally — no topic change without a Section Header

### 4. White / Standard

- **Usage:** Text-heavy slides, bullet points, complex tables, charts
- **Description:** Full white background, navy text
- **Priority:** LOW — avoid overuse. Only when dense text requires full width
- **Rule:** Never create a sequence of more than 2 Standard slides without a visual break

### 5. Global / Map

- **Usage:** Global footprint, locations, international strategy
- **Text Placement:** Right 30% only — avoid covering the map illustration
- **Description:** World map or regional map as background, text overlay in right column

### 6. Strategic Pillars / Quotes

- **Usage:** Key messages, quotes, strategic pillars (Improve / Grow / Accelerate)
- **Description:** Reverse sidebar — large deep blue content area (70%) left, vertical photo strip (30%) right
- **Text Placement:** Left 70%

### 7. Split — Aerial (Infrastructure)

- **Usage:** HIGH PRIORITY — Overview, infrastructure, logistics topics
- **Description:** Left white panel with text, right panel with aerial industrial photography
- **Text Placement:** Strict left 50%

### 8. Split — Operations (Technical)

- **Usage:** HIGH PRIORITY — Operational excellence, technical, maintenance topics
- **Description:** Left white panel with text, right panel with operations photography (tanks, pipes, terminals)
- **Text Placement:** Strict left 50%

### 9. Split — Portrait (People / Safety)

- **Usage:** HIGH PRIORITY — Safety, people, culture topics ("Vopak Heroes")
- **Description:** Left white panel with text, right panel with worker portrait photography
- **Text Placement:** Strict left 50%

### 10. Sidebar (Agenda)

- **Usage:** Agendas, step-by-step lists, content with supporting notes
- **Description:** 75/25 vertical columns — main content left, supporting points right
- **Text Placement:** Left 75% for main body, right 25% for sidebar notes

### 11. Closing Slide

- **Usage:** ONLY for the final Q&A or thank-you slide
- **Description:** Template has embedded "Thank You" — no text overlay needed
- **Rule:** NO TEXT OVERLAY. Output an empty content block.

---

## Visual Variety Rule (CRITICAL)

When planning a presentation, apply these rules to avoid visual monotony:

1. **Aggressively prioritize Split Backgrounds** (Aerial, Operations, Portrait) whenever text allows
2. **Never use more than 2 Standard/White slides in a row**
3. **Always insert a Section Header before a topic change**
4. **Alternate between dark (deep blue) and light (white/split) slides** for visual rhythm
5. **Use Strategic Pillars layout for the most important message** in the deck

## Typography Reminders

- **Font:** Inter, sans-serif for ALL text
- **No Bold Rule:** Bold is PROHIBITED for titles, headers, subtitles
- **Line Height:** Keep bullet points concise to respect 1.5 line spacing
- **Hierarchy:** Large title → medium subtitle → compact body bullets
